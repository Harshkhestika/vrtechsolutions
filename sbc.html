About SBC

Manufacturers of Quality PCC Poles

Shri Balaji Construction (SBC) is a leading manufacturer of high-quality PCC poles for both government and private sectors. We specialize in producing durable and reliable poles using concrete, cement, and other premium materials.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility located in Riico Industrial Area, Reengus Parsamrampura, is equipped with modern machinery and operated by skilled technicians to ensure the highest quality standards.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction. Our products are known for their strength, durability, and competitive pricing.
